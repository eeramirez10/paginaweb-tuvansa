Butt-weld Fittings

Used in oil and gas, petrochemical, construction, electrical, mining, sugar, hydraulic, food, paper, and pharmaceutical industries among others.

Fittings

Carbon steel butt-weld fittings manufactured byTenaris and other global mills according to ASTM A-234 WPB. Sizes ranging from ½” to 48” in STD and XS as well as all special schedules.

Stainless Steel butt-weld fittings in ASTM A-403 T304, T316L and other alloys.

Carbon steel flanges in ASTM A-105 and ANSI-B 16.9 from 1/2" to 48” in class 150, 300, 600, 900, and 1500. We carry all types including Slip On, Weld Neck, Blind, Threaded, and Socket weld among others. We also carry all the same flanges in stainless steel under spec ASTM A-182 F304L, F316L, and other alloys.

Fittings and accessories in HDPE (High Density Polyethylene).
