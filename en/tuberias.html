Pipe

Seamless pipe manufactured by TAMSA / TENARIS in ASTM A-106, A-53B, API-5L GR B, Nace, etc. We stock from ½” to 24” in all grades and wall thickness.

Welded pipe in ASTM A-53 Grade A and B, SAW, DSAW, ERW ranging from ½” to 60” in all grades and wall thickness.

Seamless and welded stainless pipe in ASTM A-312 type 304, 316, and other alloys. We also carry stainless tubing in the same types and grades.

Thin wall welded pipe From SPS Flow / Seah Steel and from Villacero in schedule 7, schedule 10, and BLT all with UL / FM approvals.

Alloy seamless pipe adhering to ASTM A-333 in grade P5, P9, P11, P22, and P91 among others.

HDPE Pipe (High Density Polyethylene) used in many industries including mining, water, etc.

Carbon steel Hollow tube with Tamsa specs TAM-245 and TAM-470 from 32mm to 508mm.
