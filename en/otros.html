Fire Protection Systems and Other Services

VICTAULIC
We are an authorized distributor of VICTAULIC brand grooved fittings and accessories, the global market leader for fire protection systems and air conditioning. Within this product line, we carry grooved fittings, sprinklers, butterfly valves, and alarm valves. All these products carry UL and FM approvals.

These materials are also used in the HVAC industry, industrial water applications, water, gasoline, water treatment plants, mining, and potable water lines.

Grooved systems provide savings in total cost, installation times, and maintenance.
