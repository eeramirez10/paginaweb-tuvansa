Tuvansa has 5 stocking warehouses within Mexican borders conveniently located in: Mexico City, Monterrey, Veracruz, Mexicali, and Queretaro.

We have more than 50,000 sq. mts (500,000 sq ft) of warehouse space stocked with a balanced inventory ensuring our customers receive service that is both competitive and professional.

Who we are

Since the company’s founding in 1956, Tuberia y Valvulas de Norte S.A de C.V. (Tuvansa) has been a leader commercializing pipe, valves, fittings, and hollow bar. For more than 60 years, the company has overcome industry related challenges, growing its position and prestige in both national and international markets.

Tuvansa’s values are: business opportunity, professionalism, quality, care of the environment, innovation, competence, enthusiasm, honesty and punctuality. All members of our organization are proud to be an integral part of the company, recognizing that Tuvansa is a close knit family.

We are a world renowned company in the steel industry, with an important position in the marketplace, constantly driving growth, and incorporating novel ideas, new products, and the latest technologies.

Our goal is total customer satisfaction and full commitment to solving all client needs.
