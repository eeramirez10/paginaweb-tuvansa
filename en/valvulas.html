Valves

We carry carbon steel valves including Gates, Globes, Checks, Ball, Butterfly, Grooved, Duo Check, Diaphragm, Knife Gates, Needle, and Pinch valves among others. We not only carry valves in carbon steel but also in stainless, alloys, and cast iron. We carry flanged, threaded, SW, and grooved ends. Also, we carry plastic valves in PVC and CPVC.

The main brands that we offer are:

WALWORTH
Gate, Globe, and Check valves in cast iron, cast steel, and forged steel. Also, API 6D ball valves, API 6D Gates valves, Plug Valves, and Duo Checks. Our valve sizes range from ¼” to 48” in class 125 through 2500 in all variations.

BRAY
Carbon steel, stainless steel, and cast iron Butterfly valves in wafer, lug, and flanged versions. We carry high performance butterfly valves in carbon and stainless steel with Teflon seats from 2” to 56”. We offer valves with manual operation, gear operation, and both pneumatic and electric actuators. We carry a great variety of valve accessories and Orbinox knife gate valves too.

HAITIMA
Carbon steel and stainless steel ball valves for many uses such as water, air, steam, and sanitary applications. Sizes range from ¼” to 12” from 800 to 2000 WOG pressures with manual operation and pneumatic or electric actuators. Wafer and lug style butterfly valves in iron and stainless steel with EPDM seats, manual or gear operated. Stainless steel gates, globes, and checks with threaded or flanged ends.

OTHER BRANDS
We represent and carry many brands such as Worcester, Velan, Genebre, Sharpe, FNW, Nibco, Mueller, Kenedy, Clarkson, Lavsa, SWI, Bonney Forge, Powell, and DSL among others
